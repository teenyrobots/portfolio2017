Onlina News Association

Print Designer

This year I designed a series of print collateral pieces for the Online News Association, including their cover for the ONA16 conference in Denver, CO. I also designed the conference's t-shirt and tote bag, as well as table materials for ONA Local.

For this project I helped ONA identify the feelings and ideas they wanted to capture with this material, including the spirit of collaboration and excitement about being together as a community in Denver. Ultimately we landed on a playful, colorful, and crafty aesthetic celebrating the hard work and devotion to community from ONA members.
