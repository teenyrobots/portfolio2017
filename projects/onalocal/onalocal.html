Event Collateral (Print)

Client: Online News Association

The Online News Association has a robust network of local association chapters across the world. ONA was looking to support their local chapters with business cards, directional signs, and promotional postcards to gain and retain chapter participation.

These collateral pieces were designed to be used by chapter leaders with little or no design/technical expertise. The directional signs and postcards were delivered as .pdfs ready for the printer. The business cards and event posters I designed in InDesign, but delivered as Microsoft Word templates for ease of use.
